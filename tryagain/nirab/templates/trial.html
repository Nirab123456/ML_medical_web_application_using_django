BANGLA OPTICAL CHARECTERS RECOGNITION

Bangla OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology is employed for the conversion of printed or handwritten Bangla text within images into digital, machine-readable text. The PyTesseract Python library serves as a tool to harness the capabilities of the Tesseract OCR engine for Bangla OCR purposes. Through PyTesseract, developers are empowered to seamlessly integrate this technology into their Python applications, facilitating automated extraction and processing of Bangla text from images. This advancement significantly contributes to improved accessibility and streamlined data management for the Bangla language.
